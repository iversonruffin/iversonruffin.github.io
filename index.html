Mission Statement

The Hidden Genius Project trains and mentors black male youth in technology creation, entrepreneurship, and leadership skills to transform their lives and communities. Throughout this program, Geniuses, as we call them, will develop relationships with their brothers and mentors that transcend the learning space.

Baltimore 2 (BAL2) is the latest cohort learning and applying their skills to develop exciting projects and experiences. Check out what Baltimore's Geniuses have brought to the table!
